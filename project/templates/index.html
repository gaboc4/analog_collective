Analog Collective

Self-Service Spotify Promotion

For Curators


Discover the next wave of up and coming artists while monetizing your playlist. Our service will analyze your playlist for the artists you feature, and this will allow independent artists to find your playlist and submit for your review.

Open up a dialogue after placements with the artists that you discover, and continue to feature them and help grow their audience, while you grow yours too. There’s no better way to earn money for simply discovering new music.

For Artists


Founded by a musician and producer, Analog Collective grew out of a need for a trustworthy tool that artists can use to grow their audience on Spotify. If you’re here, you’ve probably already spent money on music promotion with little to show for it. 

With Analog Collective, you’ll have the unique ability to search our database for playlists that have followers from your target demographic. Our self-service platform allows YOU to do decide where you hope to have your music placed, and which artists you’d like to see yourself curated beside.
